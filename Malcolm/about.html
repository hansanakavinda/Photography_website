Hello, I’m Malcolm Lismore, a passionate photographer based on the North West coast of Scotland. With the rugged beauty of the Scottish landscape as my backdrop, I’ve spent years capturing the intricate details of nature, wildlife, and the stunning coastal scenery around me. Photography is not just my career—it's a way of expressing my love for the natural world.

Wildlife Photography

Event Photography

From weddings to special events, I aim to preserve authentic moments with an artistic touch.

Portraits

Personal, family, or corporate portraits that reflect true personality and emotion.
